Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OpenQR handles privacy when You use the Service.

OpenQR is designed to work without collecting, storing, tracking, selling, sharing, or transmitting Your Personal Data. The Application does not require an account, does not use analytics, does not use advertising trackers, and does not collect Usage Data.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect any Personal Data from You when You use OpenQR.

OpenQR does not ask You to create an account and does not collect information such as Your name, email address, phone number, address, contacts, location, identifiers, or payment information.

No Usage Data Collected

We do not collect Usage Data.

OpenQR does not collect Your IP address, device identifiers, operating system details, browser information, diagnostic data, crash analytics, app activity, scan history, or any other usage information.

Camera Permission

OpenQR may request access to Your Device camera only to scan QR codes.

Camera access is used locally on Your Device. We do not collect, store, upload, transmit, or share camera images, video frames, scanned content, or QR code results.

You can enable or disable camera access at any time through Your Device settings.

Scanned QR Content

QR code scanning is processed on Your Device.

We do not collect, store, upload, transmit, share, or analyze the content of QR codes You scan.

If You choose to open a scanned link, search a scanned result, or interact with a third-party website or application, that action may be handled by Your browser, operating system, search engine, or another third-party service. Their own privacy policies may apply.

No Accounts or Social Login

OpenQR does not provide account registration, login, or Third-Party Social Media Service authentication.

No Advertising or Analytics

OpenQR does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking technologies, or behavioral profiling tools.

Use of Your Personal Data

Because We do not collect Personal Data, We do not use Your Personal Data for any purpose.

Sharing of Your Personal Data

Because We do not collect Personal Data, We do not sell, rent, trade, disclose, or share Your Personal Data with anyone.

Retention of Your Personal Data

Because We do not collect Personal Data, We do not retain Your Personal Data.

Transfer of Your Personal Data

Because We do not collect Personal Data, We do not transfer Your Personal Data to any organization, server, country, or third party.

Disclosure of Your Personal Data

Because We do not collect Personal Data, We have no Personal Data to disclose.

Law Enforcement

We cannot provide Personal Data that We do not collect. However, We may comply with applicable legal obligations if required by law.

Security of Your Personal Data

OpenQR is designed to avoid collecting Personal Data. Since We do not collect, store, or transmit Your Personal Data, there is no Personal Data stored by Us to secure.

Children's Privacy

OpenQR does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

If You believe that a child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us. If We become aware that We have received Personal Data from a child, We will take steps to delete that information.

Links to Other Websites

We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites, applications, browsers, search engines, or services. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every third-party service You visit or use.
